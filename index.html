Our #1 Top Recipe

Rendang is an Indonesian culinary masterpiece renowned for its rich flavors. This slow-cooked beef dish combines tender meat with a harmonious blend of spices and coconut milk, resulting in a savory, slightly sweet, and slightly spicy sensation. Its complexity and depth make it a beloved and iconic dish in Indonesian cuisine.

Choose your favorite menu

Serabi

Serabi is a delightful Indonesian pancake known for its small, fluffy, and slightly sweet texture. These bite-sized pancakes are traditionally made with coconut milk and rice flour, giving them a distinctively rich and coconutty flavor.

Lemper

Lemper is a popular Indonesian snack that features sticky rice cooked with coconut milk, wrapped in a banana leaf. Inside, you'll find a tasty filling, often made with seasoned shredded chicken.

Nastar

Nastar refers to delicious Indonesian pineapple tarts that are small, buttery, and melt-in-your-mouth. These bite-sized treats are typically filled with sweet pineapple jam and often enjoyed during festive occasions and celebrations.

Opor Ayam

Opor Ayam is a beloved Indonesian chicken dish known for its creamy coconut milk sauce infused with aromatic spices. Often enjoyed with steamed rice on special occasions, it's a cherished Indonesian comfort food.
